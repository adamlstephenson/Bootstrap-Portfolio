About Me

Hello, and thank you for visiting my portfolio. I decided to begin my coding journey in the fall of 2017 and have enjoyed every minute of it, minus a major frustration or two. My ultimate goal is to graduate from my boot camp program with the tools and knowledge to become a full-stack developer.
